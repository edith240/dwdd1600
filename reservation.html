Reservations

At Los Milagros Flower Farm, we invite you to embark on a delightful journey through our vibrant fields of bloom. Whether you're here to pick your own flowers, join a guided farm tour, or celebrate a special occasion, reserving your spot is quick and easy. Simply select your experience, choose a date and time, and let us help you create lasting memories surrounded by natures beauty. Book your visit today and immerse yourself in the colorful wonders of our flower farm!

How to Reserve Your Visit to Los Milagros Flower Farm:

  1. Select Your Experience
    • Pick Your Own Flowers: Enjoy a hands-on experience in our flower fields.
    • Farm Tour Take a guided tour to learn more about our sustainable farming practices.
    • Private Events or Photoshoots Book our farm for special occasions such as birthdays, weddings, or photography sessions.
    • Workshops & Classes: Participate in flower-arranging workshops, sustainability talks, or seasonal events.
  2. Choose a Date and Time
    • Use our calendar tool to select an available date for your visit. You can view the availability of each experience and see peak bloom periods.
    • Please note that certain flowers are seasonal, so check what will be in bloom during your visit.
  3. Select Group Size
    • Indicate the number of people in your group. We offer group rates for larger parties, and children under a certain age may have discounted or free admission.
  4. Customize Your Experience
    • For special events or photoshoots, you can add extra services such as:
      • Professional floral arrangements
      Custom flower picking baskets
    • Let us know if you have any special requests to make your experience more personalized.
  5. Pricing and Payment Options
    • Check the total cost for your reservation based on your experience and group size.
    • A deposit may be required for certain reservations, such as private events or large group bookings.
    • We accept credit cards, PayPal, or direct bank transfers for your convenience.
  6. Provide Contact Informatio
    • Fill in your contact details so we can confirm your booking and send any additional information you might need before your visit.
    • Make sure to include your email address and phone number.
  7. Confirm Your Reservation
    • Review all details of your booking before submitting your reservation.
    • Once confirmed, you'll receive a confirmation email with your reservation details, ticket or voucher (if applicable), and a map to help you find us.
  8. Check-In on Arrival
    • Upon arrival at Los Milagros Flower Farm, present your confirmation email or booking reference at the entrance.
    • If you've booked a special event or workshop, we'll provide further instructions on where to meet and any additional details.
