About Us

Los Milagros Flower Farm is for all flower lovers alike who want to be a part of the local flower movement and create fun everlasting memories!

Our Mission

Our mission at Los Milagros Flower Farm is to provide you with an experience of enjoyment, hope, and peace. Everything we grow from field to floral is grown from a seed of love. We hope you enjoy your experience with Los Milagros Flower Farm whether that be in the field picking flowers, a floral arrangement sitting on your table, an experience in one of our workshops, or a bouquet from our roadside stand, we hope you can feel the love from our field to your home bringing you hope and peace in your home and relationships. Come enjoy a Self-Serve Flower Farm!
