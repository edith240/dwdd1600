About Us

Welcome to Los Milagros Flower Farm, a floral sanctuary where nature's beauty thrives and guests are invited to experience it firsthand. Nestled in a breathtaking landscape, our farm cultivates a wide variety of vibrant flowers, from graceful roses to radiant sunflowers, all grown with sustainable, eco-friendly practices. Whether you're seeking fresh blooms for your home or planning a special event, Los Milagros Farm offers an unforgettable floral experience.

One of the unique joys of visiting our farm is the opportunity to pick your own flowers. Stroll through our fields, surrounded by nature's colorful creations, and hand-select the blooms that speak to you. It's a perfect way to connect with nature, create personalized bouquets, and enjoy the peaceful beauty of our farm.

At Los Milagros Farm, we are passionate about nurturing the environment while offering our guests a memorable and interactive experience. Discover the magic of fresh flowers, grown with care and love, and take home a piece of that beauty with you.

Our Services

Pick Your Own Flowers
Wander through our colorful fields and hand-pick the flowers that speak to you. Whether you're building a bouquet for your home or gathering flowers for a special event, this interactive experience allows you to connect with nature while creating something beautiful.
Seasonal Workshops
We host a variety of workshops throughout the year, offering hands-on experiences like flower arranging, wreath-making, and gardening tips. Learn new skills from experts and take home your own floral creations, perfect for brightening up your space or sharing with loved ones.
Guided Farm Tours
Explore the behind-the-scenes magic of Los Milagros Flower Farm on one of our guided tours. Learn about sustainable farming practices, the varieties of flowers we grow, and the history of our farm as you stroll through our picturesque fields.
Private Events & Photoshoots
Host your next celebration at our farm! Whether it's a wedding, birthday party, or corporate event, we offer a stunning backdrop of flower fields and personalized floral arrangements to make your day unforgettable.
